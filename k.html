Purpose of the Journey

This journey from July to December is a sacred commitment to seek God's face with intentionality. Through fasting, daily scripture, prayer, and reflection, we aim to deepen our relationship with the Lord, grow in spiritual discipline, and align our lives with His divine purpose.

September centers on cultivating intimacy with God and building spiritual strength. Draw near to Him, rely on His power, and grow in steadfast faith, allowing His presence to shape your daily life.
