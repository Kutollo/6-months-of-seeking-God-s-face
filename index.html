Purpose of the Journey

This journey from July to December is a sacred commitment to seek God's face with intentionality. Through fasting, daily scripture, prayer, and reflection, we aim to deepen our relationship with the Lord, grow in spiritual discipline, and align our lives with His divine purpose. Each month will focus on a theme rooted in God's Word, guiding us to live with faith, purpose and love. Let this season be a spiritual reset and revival for your soul.

August focuses on turning back to God with humble hearts, walking in obedience and aligning with His Word. Let this month be one of repentance, renewal, and redirection.
