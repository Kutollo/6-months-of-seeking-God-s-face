Purpose of the Journey

This website exists to guide you through a 6-month personal journey of seeking God's face through intentional spiritual disciplines. Rooted in the teachings and lifestyle of Jesus Christ, this plan will help you deepen your relationship with God, grow in the Word, engage in consistent prayer, and live with purpose and spiritual clarity.

Welcome to the Journey

This 6-month plan is a personal and powerful commitment to seek God's face through daily prayer, verse journaling, fasting, and living like Jesus Christ. Each month carries a theme, key scriptures, and journal prompts.

You're encouraged to download the journal and add the calendar to stay organized and focused.
