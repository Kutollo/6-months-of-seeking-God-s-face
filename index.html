July – December 2025

Theme: Purity, repentance, surrender

August: Identity in Christ

Scriptures: Ephesians 1–2, Romans 8

Theme: Know who you are in Christ

Action: Declare daily affirmations

Scriptures: Psalm 119, John 10

Theme: Hear and obey God's voice

Action: 30-day Bible reading challenge

October: Spiritual Warfare & Authority

Scriptures: Ephesians 6, Luke 10:19

Theme: Break strongholds

Action: Create a family warfare prayer

November: Purpose & Direction

Scriptures: Jeremiah 29:11–13

Theme: Seek God’s will for your life

Action: Write your 2026 vision statement

December: Kingdom Mindset & Impact

Scriptures: Matthew 6, Isaiah 61

Theme: Live for eternity

Action: Sow into someone’s life
